View from the hotel 18th Nov 2017. Hope you like bridges!

The reception will be held at the Hilton Queensferry Crossing over the water in North Queensferry (formerly the Queensferry Hotel). It's about 10 minutes drive from the church.

There's ample parking at the hotel, with disabled accessible spaces at the front of the building. You'll be entering via the function suite entrance and heading to the 2nd floor. There's a lift immediately through the door as you go in.

From the south:
  1. After going over the new road bridge, take the first junction (1b) off the M90 motorway towards Rosyth / North Queensferry.
  2. Take the first exit on the roundabout.
  3. Head left at the traffic lights (B981).
  4. The road sweeps under the motorway. The hotel is the first left after that point after you crest the hill.
From the north:
  1. From the M90 motorway come off junction 1b at Inverkeithing / Ferrytoll park and ride.
  2. At the roundabout, take the third exit towards Rosyth / North Queensferry, and follow from direction 3 above.
